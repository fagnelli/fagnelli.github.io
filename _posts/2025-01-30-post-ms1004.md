🔋🔋🔋Are you doing research on modeling for electrical batteries, fuel cells and other energy storage systems? 🔋🔋🔋

Consider sharing your work at the 18th U.S. National Congress on Computational Mechanics (USNCCM18) to be held in Chicago next July!

This minisymposium "Multiscale and multiphysics Modeling for Energy Storage Systems" aims to bring together researchers and practitioners working on cutting-edge multiscale and multiphysics modeling with direct applications to energy storage systems, such as batteries and fuel cells. It provides a platform for exchanging ideas, presenting novel developments, and discussing recent advances in this exciting field.

Do not wait! You can submit an abstract by January 15, 2025 by clicking at the link below and selecting MS1004: [https://lnkd.in/efne2sd6](https://lnkd.in/efne2sd6)

You contact me by direct message if you have any questions or require further information. I look forward to seeing you in Chicago!
