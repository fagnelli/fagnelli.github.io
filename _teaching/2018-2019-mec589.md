16 h • class of 12 students, supervised 4 students

Supervised four lab sessions of four hours each, during which a group of students carried out the characterization of smart materials, such as stimuli-responsive materials (shape memory alloys, piezoelectrics) or materials exhibiting unusual mechanical responses (auxetics).

Under my supervision, the students learned how to operate a universal testing machine and to use optical and thermal cameras to obtain full-field measurements (displacements, temperatures).
