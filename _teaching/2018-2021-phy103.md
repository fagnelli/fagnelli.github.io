30 h per year • class of 76 students, organised in 4 groups

Four distinct lab sessions of 4 hour duration. Students will learn basic experimental techniques, data analysis and interpretation, and documentation of experimental work. The course covers harmonic oscillators, forces and equilibrium, kinematics and collisions, and waves.
