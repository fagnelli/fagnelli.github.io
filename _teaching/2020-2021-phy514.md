20 h • supervised a group of 2 students

Supervision of the project “Autonomous Deployment of a Space Cellular Antenna: Design, Additive Manufacturing, and Mechanical Testing.” During the project, students carried out a full design cycle for a deployable antenna. Under my guidance, they learned to:

(1) simulate advanced cellular materials using finite element analysis;

(2) convert a geometric model into G-code for an FFF 3D printer;

(3) operate a universal testing machine.
